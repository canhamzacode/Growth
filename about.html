My Professional Story

My journey kicked off in Sydney, Australia, where I completed my degrees in Business and Computer Science. It was during those early days that I realized the transformative power of the internet. After completing my studies, I moved to San Francisco to explore the vast opportunities in tech. There, I specialized in user experience design and began my journey into the world of growth marketing.

In my time in Silicon Valley, I had the privilege of working with renowned tech giants. I spent several years at Survey.com, followed by a transformative 4 ½ years at eBay, where my passion for all aspects of design took root. At eBay, I served as Senior Manager of Product Strategy, leading a team that shaped product roadmaps to meet user needs.

I later joined the pioneering startup Yola.com as a Senior Product Manager. During my time there, I contributed to the evolution of the Yola user experience. Upon returning to Sydney in 2010, I built and led the Product and User Experience teams at Yola's Cape Town office.

In December 2010, I joined kalahari.com, an online retailer, where I led the expansion of the design and product management teams. In June 2012, I made the exciting transition into full-time consulting with Flow Interactive.

Fast forward to 2022 when my family and I packed our bags for Portland, Oregon. I took on the role of Director of Product at HealthSparq, a healthcare transparency startup. In 2014, I published a book titled "Making It Right: Product Management For A Startup World," sharing my insights on effective product management strategies.

I continued to explore the world of product design, working at Jive Software for a year and a half, followed by six enriching years at Wildbit, contributing to fantastic products like Postmark. In 2022, Postmark and DMARC Digests were acquired by ActiveCampaign.

In September 2023, I embarked on a new adventure as the Head of Product at Jeli.io, an incident management platform.
